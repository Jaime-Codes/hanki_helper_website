How To Use

  • AnkiConnect & AnkiWeb installation

    Anki is a flashcard program that uses spaced repetition for more efficient studying. Visit their website to learn more.

    Learn more about AnkiWeb

    Hanki Helper uses AnkiConnect to sync your drafts into Anki - AnkiConnect runs locally on your computer. Cards are only sent when you click Sync

    1. 1. Open Anki
    2. 2. Go to Tools → Add-ons → Get Add-ons
    3. 3. Enter the code: 2055492159
    4. 4. Restart Anki
    Learn more about AnkiConnect

  • Popup view

    • - Toggle Quick Drafts on or off using the slider
    • - Open the Side Panel
    • - Click online/offline icon to check Anki connection

  • Quick draft View

    1. 1. highlight text and right click.
    2. 2. copy text to deck, front or back
    3. 3. Save to SidePanel once card is completely filled out

  • Side Panel View

    Use the side panel to create cards without leaving the browser. Edit and refine drafts before syncing to Anki. Review Anki stats and streaks
