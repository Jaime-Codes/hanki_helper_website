Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Hanki Anki Helper – Draft Notes for Anki browser extension (the “Extension”) for Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox, created by Hanki_Helper (the “Service Provider”) as a free service. The Extension is provided “AS IS.”

Information Collection and Use

The Extension does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on external servers.

Any data created while using the Extension (such as draft notes, user input, or settings) is stored locally within the user’s browser using browser-provided storage mechanisms and is used solely to provide the core functionality of the Extension.

The Service Provider does not have access to this data.

Location Information

The Extension does not collect precise real-time location information from your device.

Third-Party Access

The Extension does not share data with third parties. No user data is sold, rented, or disclosed to advertisers, analytics providers, or other external services.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all use of the Extension by uninstalling it at any time through your browser’s standard extension management tools.

Children

The Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children, and the Extension does not transmit or store user data on external servers.

If a parent or guardian believes a child has used the Extension and has concerns, they may contact the Service Provider for assistance.

Security

The Service Provider is committed to protecting user privacy. Because the Extension does not transmit or store data on external servers, all data remains under the user’s control within their browser environment.
